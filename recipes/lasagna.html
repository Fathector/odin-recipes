Lasagna

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Marscapone Cheese
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Mince
  • White onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  1. Fry off your mince with your onion over medium heat
  2. In the meantime, melt the marscapone cheese and season as desired
  3. Add the red sauce to the mince
  4. In a baking dish, layer red sauce, lasagna sheets, and white sauce
  5. Top with cheeder cheese, or mozzarella if preferred
